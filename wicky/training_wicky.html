Training

Remember 3 Points!

[10minutes] This is maximum time that the dog can concentrate.
[Stop the training] If your dog can't concentrate at training.
[Like a play] Interest to training is most important point to dog.

(1) Wait

- Facing your dog and unfold your falm. And say 'wait'.
- Move backwards one step and wati 3~5 seconds.
- If your dog waited well, give him/her a reward.
- Try to move longer gradually, and to make him/her control eating foods.

(2) Sit down

- Grab a snack in your hand.
- Let your dog sniff the snack.
- Put your hand up until your dog's hip is reached the ground.
(If you raised your hand too much, your dog will jump or cling to your legs.)
- Give him/her a reward.

(3) Walking

- Say 'Let's go' when you go on a walk with your dog. And prepare some snacks in your pocket.
- Make your dog close from your leg by controlling leash.
- If your dog go far away from you, stop to walk like a tree.
- Your dog will see you and come back to your leg. Then give him/her a reward.
